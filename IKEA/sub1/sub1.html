Bedspreads
Bedspreads add warmth to your bedroom and offer a cozier night’s sleep. Swap them out with the seasons or simply use them to add a new pattern or color to your room when the mood strikes. It’s one of the easiest ways to add a little more softness (and style) to your life.

Sleeper sofas
Solving two needs at once to save you space and money, our sleeper sofas, couch beds and futons never rest – they leave that up to you or your guests. IKEA’s collection features a variety of styles and colors, with options like pull-out beds and corner sofa armchairs. Many models even include built-in storage for bed linens as well, for an even greater space saving solution.

Beds

With storage for the things you need, so you don’t have to get up once you’re all tucked in. And built to last for years – in a style that you’ll love just as long.

Mattresses
We all sleep differently and have difference preferences, which is why IKEA features a variety of different mattresses choices in twin, full, queen or king sizes. Whether spring, latex or foam -- our mattresses are designed to offer comfort and support at affordable prices. All you need to decide on is which suits your body and budget to enjoy high quality sleep for years to come.

Pillows
